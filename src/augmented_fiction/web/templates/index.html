AI-first writing for serious writers

Write with an intelligent co-author

This project is building a new kind of writing environment: not an AI toy, not just an editor, and not just for fiction. It is designed for writers who want to work with language, structure, voice, character, and revision in a deeper way.

Not just fiction → any long-form writing

Not just AI help → co-authoring system

Not just editing → world + character + dialogue engine

Not just UI → CLI + programmable writing workflows

Today, the tool helps writers draft, extend, reshape, and manage manuscripts with AI built directly into the writing process. It is for people who want to write partly auto-generated stories, explore voices and styles, quote authors inline, generate dialogue between characters, and move fluidly between an online interface and command-line workflows.

Longer term, with funding, the project hopes to grow into a full writing platform for AI-aware authors: a serious environment for manuscript development, collaboration, structured drafting, reusable characters, style-aware generation, and programmable creative workflows.

Future directions

  • Multilingual support
  • User management and collaborative workspaces
  • Export-ready manuscript workflows for submission to publishers
  • Advanced character, world, and dialogue systems
  • Better tooling for long projects, research, and revision management
